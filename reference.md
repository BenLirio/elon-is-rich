## Seconds calibration (pure arithmetic)
- 1 million s = **11.57 days** · 1 billion s = **31.7 years** · 1 trillion s = **31,688 years** (1e12 ÷ 31,557,600 s/yr). Lascaux cave paintings ≈ 17,000 years old.
